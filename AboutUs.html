About us

Heart of Garden, a Durban-based nursery, was established in 2021 by Chanelle BÃ¶siger. After being retrenched she decided to take another career path and opened this nursery that specialises in indigenous flowering plants.

The decision to do so was based on the fact that she wanted to create awareness about and positively contribute to our world's ecosystem.

Bee and other insect populations are decreasing fast due to our impact on the environment. One way to contribute to creating a better future is by planting indigenous flowering plants.

Not only do flowering plants create a colourful and cheerful home environment that improves your mood, they also:

  • Attract bees
  • Attract other insects, such as butterflies and ladybird beetles
  • Attract birds
  • Carbon dioxide is absorbed and more oxygen is released into the atmosphere
  • Regulate water cycles
